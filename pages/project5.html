Project Details :

  • Name : BMI Calculator
  • About : The BMI Calculator Desktop Application is a simple yet effective tool for calculating Body Mass Index (BMI) based on user-provided height and weight inputs. It offers a quick and convenient way to assess an individual's body weight status and categorizes it into normal, overweight, underweight, or obese levels.
